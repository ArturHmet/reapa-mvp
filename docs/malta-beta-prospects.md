## Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Warm Introductions (Week 1-2)
1. Join Malta Property Agents Club on Facebook
2. Attend MDA events/networking
3. Direct outreach via LinkedIn to individual agents
4. Cold email to mid-size agency managers

### Phase 2: Demo Presentations (Week 3-4)
1. Schedule 1:1 demos with Tier 2 agency managers
2. Free trial offers for Tier 3 solo agents
3. MDA presentation proposal

### Phase 3: Beta Program (Week 5-12)
1. Onboard 10-20 beta testers (mix of solo and agency agents)
2. Weekly feedback calls
3. Iterate on highest-impact features
4. Collect testimonials for marketing
